RBras Bulletin

RBras publishes a bulletin containing news about member activities, letters to the editor and comments from members and editorials. It provides a means for expressing ideas about statistical and biometric issues and is published biannually. If you would like to submit content to the Bulletin, please contact one of the editors. The RBras Bulletin is just available in Portuguese.
