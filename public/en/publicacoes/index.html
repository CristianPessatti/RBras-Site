RBras Bulletin

RBras publishes a newsletter containing news about its activities, letters to the editor and comments from the members and editorials. It provides a means to express ideas about statistical and biometric issues and is published biannually. If you would like to submit content to the RBras Bulletin, please contact one of the editors.
